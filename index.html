Welcome to the Greenwich String Quartet

Wonderful Music For Every Occasion

The Greenwich String Quartet is a highly regarded professional ensemble, playing in many prestigious venues in London, Kent and the South East. Its members are experienced musicians and perform regularly with London's leading orchestras.

The quartet members studied at the Guildhall School of Music and Drama, the Royal Academy of Music and the Royal Northern College of Music, and play with many of London's orchestras including the Royal Philharmonic Orchestra, BBC Concert Orchestra, London Concert Orchestra and London Mozart Players. We have performed to royalty and celebrities, and worked with major figures in the music world, such as Placido Domingo, Yehudi Menuhin, Nigel Kennedy and Luciano Pavarotti.
